About Us

As a collective of Black students at the University of Toronto Scarborough (UTSC) within the BBA Management program, we have joined together with the aim of targeting the current underrepresentation of Black youth in our school. As students in different specialists and grade levels within the program, we desire a positive change in the number of members of the Black community within our program.
